enum class WordFixNotation : uint8_t
strong

The smallest unit is a word. A word comes with a "prefix" and a "postfix" and a byte (flag) that signals which to apply:

  • 0000 (not initialised)
  • 0010 (word)
  • 0110 (prefix & word)
  • 0011 (word & postfix)
  • 0111 (prefix, word, postfix)
